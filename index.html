Preparing to evacuate

  1. Tune in the radio or television for volcano updates
  2. Listen for disaster sirens and warning signals
  3. Review your emergency plan and gather your emergency supplies. Be sure to pack at least a 1-week supply of prescription medications
  4. Prepare an emergency kit for your vehicle with food, flares, booster cables, mapst, tools, a first adi kit, a fire extingusher, sleeping bags, a flashlight, batteries, etc.
  5. Fill your vehicle's gas mask
  6. If no vehicle is available, make arrangement with friends or family for transportation, or follows authorities' instructions on where to obtain transportation.
  7. Place vehicles under cover, if at all possible.
  8. Put livestock in an enclosed area. Plan ahead to take pets with you, nut be aware that many emergency shelters cannot accept animals
  9. Fill your clean water containers
  10. Adjust the thermostat or refrigerators and freezers to the coolest possible temperature. If the pwer goes out, food will stay cooler longer
